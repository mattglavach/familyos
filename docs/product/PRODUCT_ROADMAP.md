# Product Roadmap

## Purpose
This document organizes the long-term product vision into pillars. It does not replace the engineering roadmap in `docs/planning/ROADMAP.md`.

## Pillar 1: Family Hub

### Dashboard
Daily morning briefing, household priorities, schedule, task load, family activity, quick add, and household insights.

### Calendar
Today and upcoming schedule, connected calendars, household events, school calendars, reminders, and future conflict detection.

### Notifications
In-app feedback first, then future reminders, notification center, push/email options, and intelligent alerts.

### Household
Members, roles, invitations, active household switching, permissions, household defaults, and future ownership recovery.

## Pillar 2: Productivity

### Tasks
Shared household tasks, chores, assignments, due dates, priorities, status, recurrence, and task history.

### Projects
Future grouped work for home projects, school planning, travel, holidays, and multi-step household efforts.

### Chores
Future recurring, age-appropriate responsibilities tied to household members.

### Routines
Future morning, evening, weekend, school, travel, and seasonal routines.

## Pillar 3: Planning

### Shopping
Release 1.2 shared grocery and household lists, shopping items, and simple pantry inventory.

### Meal Planning
Release 1.3 weekly meal plans, recipes, recipe ingredients, meal assignments, pantry-aware missing ingredient review, and confirmed Shopping list generation.

### Recipes
Release 1.3 family recipe library foundation with favorites, ingredients, tags, notes, and meal links. Dietary/nutrition intelligence remains future scope.

### Inventory
Pantry, household supplies, home assets, and future document links.

## Pillar 4: Life

### Life Lists
Release 1.1 generic collections for books, movies, gifts, bucket lists, ideas, places, activities, and family goals.

### Travel
Trips, packing, itineraries, documents, and home-prep tasks.

### Books
Reading lists and family recommendations.

### Movies
Watch lists and family preferences.

### Goals
Personal and household goals.

### Gifts
Gift ideas, holidays, birthdays, and purchase tracking.

### Bucket Lists
Experiences, travel ideas, long-term family plans.

## Pillar 5: Home

### Maintenance
Home systems, recurring maintenance, equipment history, warranties, and reminders.

### Pool
Pool Care Assistant for water health, treatment decisions, maintenance history, equipment care, seasonal readiness, and future AI coaching with human-confirmed actions.

### Yard
Garden, plants, seasonal work, and outdoor maintenance.

### Vehicles
Vehicle records, maintenance, insurance, and reminders.

### Smart Home
Future Home Assistant or smart-home status, alerts, and automations after core product trust is established.

## Pillar 6: AI

### Assistant
Natural-language help, summaries, planning support, and household Q&A.

### Automation
Future task creation, reminders, recurring workflows, and smart suggestions.

### Recommendations
Recommendations for planning, maintenance, shopping, schedules, and household decisions.

### Voice
Future hands-free capture and query.

## Major Milestones
- Foundation: auth, app shell, dashboard, tasks, settings, household data, process docs.
- Collaboration: invitations, members, roles, active household switching.
- Release 1.0: core household operating readiness.
- Post-1.0: refine navigation, calendar, notifications, and Quick Add.
- Later: nutrition preferences, Health integration, AI recommendations, external recipe APIs, documents, home maintenance, finance depth, smart home, Shopping refinements, and richer Life Lists category templates or recommendations.

## Release 1.0 Alignment
Release 1.0 supports the Family Hub and Productivity pillars only where they are already part of the core operating loop. It should not start broad Planning, Life, Home expansion, or AI automation.
